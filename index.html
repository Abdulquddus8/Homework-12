Tasks of the mentor

  • Check student assignments

    Everything is simple here. The student sends what he did. You check it and find errors. But if the task is sent, then it must be checked within 24 hours.

  • Conduct consultations with analysis of errors

    Parse mistakes with your voice. One hour per week per student. Suggest solutions and explain what and how.

  • Support a student when things are really bad

    There are times when a student is discouraged, burst into tears and is generally not well done. It is important for you not only to keep calm, but also to cheer him up.
